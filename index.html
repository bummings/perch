Events at Perch

Plant Swap @ Perch! Thursday 6/27
@ 7:30-9:00 PM

FREE to participate- just sign-up & bring 1-2 plant clippings to swap for 1-2 new ones!

This will be a ~casual~ event so feel free to show up anytime within this time frame. But keep in mind this will be first come, first serve for the drop & swap!


Chill rules:

  • Please make sure that your mother plant appears pest free and healthy before you take a clipping.
  • Transport your clipping in a cup of water or wrap the end in a wet paper towel.
  • Upon arrival, every clipping will get a name tag so everyone knows what it is (If you are unsure, it's okay to name them Bob or Cynthia).
  • You will then drop your clipping off on the clipping table and swap it for a new one!
  • Hang out in Perch and make new plant friends!


Everything in the shop will be 10% off the night of the swap!

There will be snacks and special plant-themed Cocktail AND Mocktail beverages available! (1 per person)

All plant-enthusiasts of all levels welcome!!

Return Policy

For Plants: A plant can be exchanged or returned for store credit within 7 days. This is in the case that there is an apparent pest, rot, or other health issue out of the buyer's control.

For Odds & Ends: All odds and ends can be returned for a refund, store credit, or exchanged within 14 days.
