Events at Perch

Hand Broom Workshop
Tuesday 10/1 @ 6:00 - 8:30PM
Tuesday 10/8 @ 6:00 - 8:30PM (Just added!)

In this class, you will make two brooms:

  • Mini table whisk
  • Turkey wing hand broom

This class is beginner friendly and requires no prior experience. It does require minimal hand and arm strength, and sitting in place for up to about 45 minutes. You will be able to finish both brooms in class and bring them home ready to use!

Choose your color and customize the handle by adding dried flowers and seed heads.

All supplies and tools will be provided (included in cost!)
This workshop has a run time of approx. 2.5 hours.

Perch will provide some seasonal snacks but feel free to BYO anything you might want to drink during the workshop.

Please enjoy 20% off everything at Perch on the night of the workshop!

* Only 10 slots available in each class *

Cozy Fern Forest

Sunday 9/15 @ 6:00PM

Ring in the cozy fall season by creating a woodsy fern scene planted in a cement log planter!

Return Policy

For Plants: A plant can be exchanged or returned for store credit within 7 days. This is in the case that there is an apparent pest, rot, or other health issue out of the buyer's control.

For Odds & Ends: All odds and ends can be returned for a refund, store credit, or exchanged within 14 days.
